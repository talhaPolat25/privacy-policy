1. Overview

Learn English With Games ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is an educational mobile application developed by Muhammed Talha Polat ("Developer"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information from users ("you") of our iOS application.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the data necessary to provide the App's features and do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Display Name / Username: A nickname you choose for your profile and leaderboards.
  • Email Address: Collected when you sign in with Google or Apple ID.
  • Avatar Selection: The avatar index you choose (0–11) from the in-app avatar picker.
  • Country / Flag: The country flag you select from a list of 35+ countries.
  • Language Preference: The target language and UI language you select.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • User ID (UID): A unique, anonymized identifier generated by Firebase Authentication.
  • Game Scores & Performance: Best scores, correct/incorrect answers, XP earned, and games played for each game type (TimeRace, Pasaparola, WordFill).
  • Play Activity: Daily play counts, daily XP, streak count, and last-played date.
  • Leaderboard Entries: Your score and UID submitted to global daily, weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboards.
  • Premium Status: Whether you hold an active premium subscription.
  • Profile Completion Status: A boolean indicating whether you have completed profile setup.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or media files
  • Browser history or cross-app tracking data
  • Health or biometric data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Create and manage your user account and profile.
  • Authenticate you securely via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In.
  • Track your game progress, scores, XP, and streaks.
  • Populate and display global leaderboards.
  • Enforce daily play limits based on your account tier (Guest, Free, Premium).
  • Manage and verify your in-app subscription status.
  • Moderate usernames using our profanity filter to maintain a respectful environment.
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs (using anonymized data only).

We do not use your data for:

  • Targeted or behavioral advertising
  • Selling or renting data to third parties
  • Automated decision-making that produces legal effects
  • Any purpose beyond delivering the App's core features

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they process your data:

4.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Firebase Authentication: Handles sign-in via Google and Apple ID. Stores your UID, email, and display name securely.
  • Cloud Firestore: Our cloud database. Stores your profile data, game stats, play activity, and leaderboard scores.

Data stored in Firebase is located on Google Cloud servers, primarily in the United States. Firebase complies with GDPR and maintains Standard Contractual Clauses for data transfers.
Firebase Privacy Policy →

4.2 Google Sign-In (Google LLC)

Used for OAuth-based authentication. We receive your Google account's UID, email address, and display name. We do not request access to Gmail, Google Drive, or any other Google services.
Google Privacy Policy →

4.3 Sign in with Apple (Apple Inc.)

Used for OAuth-based authentication. Apple may share a verified email address (or a private relay address) and your name. Authentication uses a cryptographically secure nonce to protect against replay attacks.
Apple Privacy Policy →

4.4 RevenueCat, Inc.

Manages in-app purchase and subscription tracking. RevenueCat receives your Apple-generated purchase receipt and a user identifier (your Firebase UID) to verify and track subscription entitlements. RevenueCat does not receive your full name, email address, or payment card details — those remain with Apple.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy →

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Firebase (Google) and RevenueCat as described above, strictly to operate the App.
  • Leaderboard Display: Your display name and score are visible to other users on in-app leaderboards. Your email address and UID are never shown publicly.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority, we may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to this Privacy Policy.

We never sell your personal data to third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

6. Data Storage & Retention

6.1 Cloud Storage (Authenticated Users)

Your account data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore. Data is retained for as long as you maintain an active account. Firestore data is encrypted at rest and in transit by Google.

6.2 Local Storage (On-Device)

The App stores the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Language preferences
  • Profile setup status (cached for performance)
  • Best scores (local cache)
  • Premium status cache
  • Daily play count tracking
  • Guest mode statistics (when not signed in)

Local data is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

6.3 Data Retention

Authenticated user data is retained in Firestore until you request account deletion (see Section 11). Guest user data exists only on-device and is not retained after uninstalling the App. Leaderboard entries are retained for the relevant period (daily: 24 hours, weekly: 7 days, monthly: calendar month, all-time: indefinitely).

7. Guest Mode

You may use the App as a guest without creating an account. In Guest Mode:

  • No personal information is collected or transmitted to our servers.
  • Your game statistics (XP, streak, best scores, play counts) are stored only on your device.
  • You are limited to 2 games per day.
  • Guest data is not backed up to the cloud and will be lost if you uninstall the App.
  • You may convert to a full account at any time; guest data is not automatically transferred.

8. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

The App offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and yearly) that unlock unlimited daily gameplay. All purchases are processed by Apple's App Store payment system. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or financial account information.

RevenueCat receives Apple purchase receipts and your Firebase UID solely to verify subscription entitlements. Your premium status (a boolean) is synced to your Firestore profile.

For refund requests or subscription management, please use your Apple ID account settings or contact Apple Support directly.

9. Device Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive device permissions, including:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location Services
  • Contacts / Address Book
  • Photos / Media Library
  • Health Data
  • Bluetooth
  • Push Notifications (optional – you will be prompted if this changes)

The only URL scheme registered in the App is used for redirecting back from Google Sign-In after OAuth authentication, which is standard OAuth behavior.

10. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is designed as an educational English-learning game and may be suitable for users of all ages. However, the App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 in the United States (as defined by COPPA) or children under 16 in the European Union (as defined by GDPR).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information without consent, please contact us immediately at the email address in Section 14, and we will promptly delete such information.

If you wish to allow a child under 13 to use the App, we recommend the Guest Mode, which does not require account creation or collection of personal information.

11. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA & Similar Laws)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may correct inaccurate or incomplete data via the in-app profile settings at any time.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You may request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

CCPA (California Residents): California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in Section 14. We will respond within 30 days (or 45 days for complex requests). We do not charge a fee for exercising your rights.

Account & Data Deletion: To delete your account and all associated data, please email us with your registered email address and the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will delete your Firestore data within 30 days. Residual data in backup systems may persist for up to 90 days before being automatically purged.

12. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and Firebase is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS).
  • Encryption at Rest: Firebase/Google Cloud encrypts all stored data at rest by default using AES-256.
  • Authentication Security: Apple Sign-In uses a cryptographically secure nonce (SHA-256) to prevent replay attacks.
  • Firebase Security Rules: Firestore database rules are configured to restrict access so users can only read/write their own data.
  • Username Moderation: An embedded profanity filter prevents offensive display names from being stored.

While we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and, if required, notify affected users without undue delay.
